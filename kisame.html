Kisame Hoshigaki

Kisame Hoshigaki was one of the strongest members of the Akatsuki.

He was originally a ninja from the Hidden Mist Village.

Kisame was known as the "Monster of the Hidden Mist" because of his massive chakra reserves.

His main weapon was the powerful sword Samehada, which could absorb chakra from enemies.

Kisame was the partner of Itachi Uchiha in the Akatsuki.

He specialized in powerful Water Style techniques that could create massive oceans during battle.

His shark-like appearance and brutal fighting style made him one of the most feared shinobi.
