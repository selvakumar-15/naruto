Obito Uchiha

Obito Uchiha was one of the most important characters behind the Akatsuki.

He was originally a member of Team Minato along with Kakashi Hatake and Rin Nohara.

After being believed dead during a mission, Obito was secretly saved by Madara Uchiha.

Obito later used the name "Tobi" while secretly controlling the Akatsuki.

He possessed the Mangekyo Sharingan ability called Kamui which allowed him to teleport and become intangible.

Obito started the Fourth Great Ninja War to achieve the Infinite Tsukuyomi plan.

Later he realized his mistakes and helped Naruto and the Allied Shinobi Forces.
