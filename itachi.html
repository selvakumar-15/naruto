Itachi Uchiha

Itachi Uchiha was one of the strongest members of the Akatsuki.

He was a prodigy of the Uchiha Clan and became an ANBU captain at a very young age.

Itachi possessed the Mangekyo Sharingan, which granted him powerful abilities.

His famous techniques include Tsukuyomi, Amaterasu, and the powerful Susanoo.

He joined the Akatsuki after leaving the Hidden Leaf Village.

In truth, Itachi sacrificed everything to protect the village and his younger brother Sasuke.

He secretly acted as a spy for the Hidden Leaf while working inside Akatsuki.

Before his death, Itachi ensured that Sasuke would grow strong enough to protect the village.
