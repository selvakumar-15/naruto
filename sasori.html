Sasori of the Red Sand

Sasori was a powerful member of the Akatsuki.

He was originally a ninja from the Hidden Sand Village.

Sasori was known as "Sasori of the Red Sand" because the battlefield would be covered in blood during his fights.

He was a master puppeteer who created extremely deadly puppet weapons.

Sasori turned his own body into a puppet to achieve immortality.

He also created the famous Third Kazekage puppet, one of his strongest weapons.

Sasori worked as Deidara’s partner in the Akatsuki.

He was eventually defeated by Sakura Haruno and Chiyo.
