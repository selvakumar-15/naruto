Deidara

Deidara was a member of the Akatsuki organization.

He was originally a ninja from the Hidden Stone Village.

Deidara possessed special mouths on his hands that allowed him to create explosive clay.

He believed that true art is an explosion and often shouted “Katsu!” before detonating his creations.

Deidara could create flying clay birds, dragons, and powerful bombs.

He was partnered with Sasori and later with Tobi in the Akatsuki.

His final battle was against Sasuke Uchiha where he used his ultimate self-destruction technique.
