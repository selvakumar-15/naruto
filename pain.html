Pain (Nagato)

Pain is the leader of the Akatsuki organization.

His real name is Nagato Uzumaki. He was a war orphan from the Hidden Rain Village.

Nagato awakened the legendary Rinnegan, one of the most powerful eyes in the Naruto world.

Using black chakra receivers, he controlled six different bodies known as the Six Paths of Pain.

Each path had a different ability such as controlling gravity, summoning creatures, or absorbing chakra.

Pain believed that true peace could only be achieved when the world experienced great suffering.

His most famous techniques include Shinra Tensei, Bansho Ten'in and Chibaku Tensei.

Later, Naruto Uzumaki convinces Nagato to believe in hope and peace again.
